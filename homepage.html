Using AI, better sustainability can be achieved in many areas,

including the aftermath of natural disasters

Artificial Intelligence can
collect the data of
various areas using
satellite imagery to
assess which areas need
assistance the most, and
adequately distribute
supplies. They can also
predict future needs in
locations based on
weather data, ground
data, and past disasters.

Some real-life
examples where drones
using AI assisted in
emergencies were
during Puerto Rico's
Hurricane Maria in 2017,
the Nepal Earthquake
of 2015, the 2019
Cyclone Idai in
Mozambique, and most
recently, the California
wildfires.
