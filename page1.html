For instance, AI can be used to allocate disaster resources to different areas based on how urgently it is needed.

The data used by the AI:
- Geospatial data
- Environmental and
weather data
- Social media feeds
- Government reports
- Medical databases

Basis on which the AI
makes choices:
- Infrastructure damage
- Population density
- Availability of food
and water in the area
- Transport networks
- Weather Conditions
- Vulnerable populations
- Disease Outbreaks
