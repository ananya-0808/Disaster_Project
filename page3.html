Here, the blockchain updates every five seconds to record

the most recent data, and make changes to the resources

available. Soon, after a certain number of iterations,

the resources will run out to make it more realistic.

Visualizing the data helps understand

how the resources are allocated by

the AI.

The AI automatically analyzes data from various sources like satellites, social media and weather data. AI can help optimize this distribution by identifying which regions are in most need, the best routes for delivery, and how to minimize waste. AI can also change or make adjustments to the current resource allocations in the scenario where something changes.

Equipped with this AI, drones and self-driving trucks can be sent to sites which require resources immediately, without the need of human intervention. This ensures that human resources can be used in more physical tasks like assistance of the injured and rescue operations.
